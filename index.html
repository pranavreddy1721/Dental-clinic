About Our Clinic

At Chougule Dental Clinic, Ashta, we are a multispecialty dental clinic known for professional, gentle ("soft hands"), quick, hygienic, and reasonably priced treatments. We focus on patient comfort and modern dental solutions.

Our clinic is led by experienced doctors Dr. Rohit Chougule and Dr. Prangha Chougule, who are highly appreciated by patients for their expertise and care.
